# How to Play Pedro

Pedro is a thrilling multiplayer card game played with a standard 52-card deck.

## The Objective
Reach the target score first to win the game.

## Game Phases

### 1. The Wadger (Bidding) Phase
- Players bid on how many points they can accumulate.
- Initial bid must be at least 1.
- Each subsequent player may increase the bid or pass.
- The highest bidder chooses the **Trump Suit**.

### 2. Discarding & Dealing
- Non-trump cards are discarded.
- Players are dealt back up to 6 cards.

### 3. Game Play Phase
- Players play one card per turn in anti-clockwise order.
- The highest trump card wins the lift.
- If no trump is played, the highest card of the lead suit wins.

## Scoring
- **High Trump:** 1 point
- **Low Trump:** 1 point
- **5 of Trumps:** 5 points
- **9 of Trumps:** 9 points
- **Jack of Trumps:** 1 point (3 points if won from an opponent)
- **Game Point:** 1 point for the player with the most cards of value (Ace, King, Queen, Jack, 10).
