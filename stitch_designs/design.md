# Design System: Mint & Marigold

## 1. Creative North Star: "The Social Veranda"
This design system departs from high-stakes, dark aesthetics to embrace the feeling of a sunny afternoon social club. It’s light, airy, and focuses on friendly competition and community. The visual language is "Delightful, Social, and Approachable."

## 2. Color Palette

### Primary & Brand
- **Mint Green (#3EB489):** Our primary brand color. Used for key interactive elements, branding, and status indicators. It feels fresh and modern.
- **Sunny Amber (#FFCA53):** Our accent color. Used for "delight" moments, primary call-to-actions, and highlighting wins (like the active tab or a win streak).

### Neutrals
- **Canvas White (#F5F7F5):** An off-white, slightly warm neutral used for backgrounds to reduce eye strain and provide a "paper-like" quality.
- **Deep Slate (#2C2F2E):** Used for primary text and high-contrast icons.
- **Soft Moss (#E8EBE8):** Used for subtle boundaries, card backgrounds, and secondary information.

## 3. Typography
We use **Plus Jakarta Sans** as our primary typeface. It's a modern geometric sans-serif that strikes a perfect balance between professional and friendly.

- **Headlines:** Bold, tracking-tight. Used for page titles and large numeric displays (like bid amounts).
- **Body:** Regular or Medium weight. Highly readable for chat and descriptions.
- **Status/Caps:** Used for labels (like "PRO PLAYER") to provide a clean, organized look.

## 4. Components & Shape
Consistency is driven by "Roundness." Most containers and buttons use a high corner radius to feel soft and inviting.

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Rounded-full, Sunny Amber background with Deep Brown text.
- **Secondary:** Rounded-full, Mint Green background with White text.
- **Ghost/Tonal:** Subtle tonal shifts with rounded corners.

### Cards & Containers
- Containers feature large corner radii (typically `2rem` or `rounded-3xl`).
- Subtle shadows and tonal layering (using "Soft Moss" on "Canvas White") create depth without feeling heavy.

## 5. Iconography
We use **Material Symbols (Rounded)**. The rounded style mirrors our component shapes, reinforcing the approachable brand personality.

## 6. Layout Principles
- **Airy Spacing:** Generous whitespace between sections.
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Use of scale (large numbers for scores/bids) and color (Amber for active states) to guide the player's eye.
- **Mobile First:** Optimized for thumb-driven navigation with a prominent bottom navigation bar.
